Place your local images in this folder. Recommended filenames for this demo:
- `hero.jpg` (hero background)
- `room1.jpg`, `room2.jpg`, `room3.jpg` (carousel images)

- The hero background referenced in the CSS is `assets/hero.jpg`.
- Replace the carousel <img> src in `index.html` with `assets/room1.jpg`, `assets/room2.jpg`, etc., or your own filenames.
